3d Printing

I uses materials like plastic, metal, or resin to "print" real, 3D items. The design is made on a computer and then sent to a 3D printer, which builds the object by adding thin layers one at a time. This technology allows quick, custom production, making it useful for prototypes, tools, toys, and even medical devices.
